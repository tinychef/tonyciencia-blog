## Education, Automation & Purpose-Driven Growth
Tony Ciencia is a brand built at the intersection of technology, education, and business strategy. Our mission is to make automation, artificial intelligence, and digital marketing accessible for entrepreneurs and companies seeking smarter, more efficient growth.
Founded by Antonio Duque—a content creator on YouTube and digital educator—Tony Ciencia is now a growing community and an active business committed to delivering real-world results through modern technology and applied knowledge.

## Mission, Vision & Core Values
#### Mission

###### 🚀
To democratize automation and AI, helping individuals and businesses grow with less effort and greater impact.

#### Vision

###### 🤝
To become the bridge between tech education and scalable business success across the Spanish-speaking world and beyond.

#### Values
Accessible learning · Transparency · Practical technology · Sustainable growth · Community

## About the Founder — Antonio Duque
My name is Antonio Duque, but many know me online as Tony Ciencia. I’m a tech educator, automation specialist, and founder of this company.
I started with a simple mission: to show that anyone—even without a programming background—can learn to automate, optimize, and grow using today’s tools.
Today, I lead a bilingual, global community of learners and business owners who are using automation not just to work smarter—but to build scalable systems and grow with clarity.
